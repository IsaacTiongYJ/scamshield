## 1. Block known scam callers
*  Open **Settings**
*  Tap **Phone**
*  Tap **Call Blocking & Identification**
*  Enable **Scamshield**

![](/images/setup-guide1.png)


## 2. Enable auto spam SMS filter
* Open **Settings**
* Tap **Messages**
* Tap **Unknown & Spam**
* Enable **Scamshield**

![](/images/setup-guide2.png)
