# 1. Block known scam callers
*  Open **Settings**
*  Tap **Phone**
*  Tap **Call Blocking & Identification**
*  Enable **Scamshield**


# 2. Enable auto spam SMS filter
* Open **Settings**
* Tap **Messages**
* Tap **Unknown & Spam**
* Enable **Scamshield**
